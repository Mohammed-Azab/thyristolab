\subsubsection{Mathematical Equations Used}
\TODO{List the specific equations implemented in code}

For firing angle $\alpha$, the average charging current is:
\begin{equation}
    I_{dc} = \frac{1}{2\pi R_{batt}} \int_{\alpha}^{\pi} (V_m \sin\theta - E_{batt}) d\theta
\end{equation}

Charging time for desired SoC change:
\begin{equation}
    t_{charge} = \frac{\Delta Q}{I_{dc}} = \frac{Q_{capacity} \cdot \Delta SoC}{I_{dc}}
\end{equation}
